# Governance and Compliance Documentation for Cipher-Tube Federal OS

## Purpose
This document outlines the governance and compliance framework for Cipher-Tube Federal OS to ensure adherence to federal regulations and effective risk management.

## Scope
This framework applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party vendors who interact with Cipher-Tube Federal OS.

## Roles and Responsibilities
- **Governance Team**: Oversee compliance activities and ensure alignment with federal regulations.
- **Project Management**: Ensure that governance policies are embedded within the project lifecycle.
- **IT Security**: Manage risk assessment and incident response procedures.

## Compliance Regulations
- Federal Information Security Management Act (FISMA)
- Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program (FedRAMP)
- Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), if applicable

## Risk Management
- Conduct regular risk assessments to identify and mitigate security vulnerabilities.
- Implement controls to protect sensitive data and ensure data integrity.

## Incident Management
- Define a clear procedure for reporting and responding to security incidents.
- Conduct regular training to prepare staff for incident response.

## Review and Updates
- This document will be reviewed annually and updated as necessary to reflect changes in regulations or operational practices.
